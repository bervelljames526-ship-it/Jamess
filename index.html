About JB'S ONE-STOP SHOP

Founded in 2023 at the University of Ghana, JB'S ONE-STOP SHOP was created to provide students and the community with access to quality men's apparel and accessories at affordable prices.

We specialize in curating the best selection of shirts, jewelry, grooming products, and phone accessories specifically tailored for the modern Ghanaian man.

Our mission is to simplify your shopping experience by offering everything you need in one convenient location, both physically at Jubilee Hall and through our online store.

We pride ourselves on excellent customer service, quality products, and building lasting relationships with our customers.

Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept mobile money (MTN & Vodafone cash), bank transfers, cash on delivery (within UG campus), and cash payments at our physical store.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery within University of Ghana campus: Same day if ordered before 2 PM. Delivery in Accra: 1-2 business days. Other regions: 3-5 business days.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! University of Ghana students with valid ID cards receive a 10% discount on all purchases at our physical store.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for unused items with original packaging. Items must be in original condition. For hygiene reasons, grooming products cannot be returned once opened.

Can I place an order via WhatsApp?

Absolutely! You can browse our catalog and place orders directly through WhatsApp at +233 53 713 7623. We'll guide you through the ordering process.
